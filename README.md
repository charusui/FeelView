# FeelView

**The private, elder-first intergenerational family network.**

---

## 🌟 Project Brief

FeelView is designed from the ground up to solve the digital isolation of elderly family members. While younger generations communicate fluidly through fast-paced messaging and social media, older relatives are often left behind due to complex interfaces, tiny text, and difficult navigation.

FeelView bridges this gap with an elder-first, highly accessible intergenerational social platform:
- **Elder-First UI/UX:** Warm green and high-contrast styling with extra-large typography, simplified actions, and haptic feedback.
- **Natural Voice Navigation:** A built-in microphone interface allowing elders to locate children, grandchildren, and relatives by simply speaking their names (e.g., *"Show me Sarah"*).
- **Interactive Family Comments:** Text-to-Speech (TTS) integration reads family messages out loud at the tap of a button. Elders can easily type and post replies or capture/share new photo memories.
- **Smart Photo Narrations:** Automated AI-powered image descriptions and context generation, ensuring elders always understand the story behind every family picture.
- **Admin Moderation Control:** A comprehensive dashboard for family administrators to manage privacy settings and easily take down inappropriate posts, comments, or chat messages.

## 🛠️ Google Technologies Used

* **Flutter** — Multiplatform application development framework for rendering accessible web and mobile interfaces.
* **Gemini API** — Generative AI engine to transform raw photo metadata, tags, and captions into natural spoken narrations.
* **Cloud Firestore** — Real-time database backend for storing family posts, comments, and instant chat thread updates.
* **Firebase Storage** — Secure cloud hosting for shared family media assets and recorded audio files.
* **Firebase Core & Auth** — Private, secure environment authentication and framework bootstrapping.
* **Google Cloud Text-to-Speech** — High-definition neural interop voices (Amy/Piper) for cloud narration playback and comment readings.
